🌟 Stargazing App Orientation Detector

Based on research from findingStars.txt

Device Orientation Data

Alpha (Yaw/Heading): °

Beta (Pitch): °

Gamma (Roll): °

Interpretation

Research-Based Logic

• Beta 0° = Device flat (screen up)

• Beta 90° = Device vertical (pointing up)

• Beta 10-90° = Pointing at sky (stars visible)

• Beta > 90° = Pointing at ground
